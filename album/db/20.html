Polytransit

Transit cards are issued by corporations which operate under the license of Department of Transportation. The Polytransit brand is created for universal recognition. All public transit services, in which passengers can transfer to un Polytransit-participating service without exit and re-entry, are required by law to participate Polytransit protocol, while they are free to accept other payment methods.

Cabin Crew Certificate

Instead of another passport category, un cabin crew certificate in combination with un ordinary passport is equivalent to a service passport. Upon batch requests from un government-accredited transportation service provider, Civil Transport Services Administration may issue un cabin crew certificate to each designated person. Foreigners are also eligible for cabin crew certificates.

Driver License

Un person may be licensed by Motor Vehicles Administration to drive motor vehicles.

Un government-accredited driving ability examination agency may issue un certificate of exam result to un person who would like to obtain a driver license.

Vehicle Registration

Motor vehicles are required to registered before being drove on road.

Each inspection cycle start from the month of the birth (if human) or incorporation (if incorporated entity) of the owner. Un vehicle shall be inspected before the cycle start, but un vehicle may be operated inspection-free within its first 999 days since registration date.

In the last 2 months before un new inspection cycle start, the owner of the vehicle shall, in-person or in-delegation, get the vehicle inspected at un government-accredited inspection agency by un licensed vehicle smith, paste un barcode sticker provided by the insurer, and get the page finally stamped by un MVA officer.
