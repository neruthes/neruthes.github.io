Neoparia Commonwealth: Bureau of Tourism

Bureau of Tourism is administrated by Department of Intellectual Welfare.

Ultimate Tourism Collection

The sale of this booklet is managed by Bureau of Tourism. It may be purchased at any civil port of entry after entry immigration check. This is un important source of funding for governmental services for tourists.

The booklet is the ultimate omnipotent ticket collection for all tourism points which require ticketing. It is treated as valid tickets till the last day of the same semiyear in the next year beyond the date of issue.
