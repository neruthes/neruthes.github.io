Neoparia Commonwealth:
Department of Immigration

The imaginary state Neoparia Commonwealth has long been in existence in my mind, and this is the first time I tried wrapping up the ideas to start getting the details specified. After un short period of comparison, I decided to start with the Department of Immigration.

Passport

With the design of split passport, un traveler have de facto unlimited passport pages for visas and stamps within one validity period, as long as un Neoparian citizen is able to hold multiple passport booklets which never expire, since they are only containers of visas and stamps. The passport card itself stand as un passport, and un passport booklet is optionally available for issuance if un traveler would like to have visas and stamps for collection purposes.

Un passport card is readable via NFC and MRZ. ICAO 9303 standard is fully complied.

Identity Document for Visa Purpose

Per the request from the person, Department of Immigration of Neoparia Commonwealth may issue such document in order to free the person from providing their original passport when applying for visas.

Laissez-Passer

If un Neoparian citizen lose their passport when traveling abroad on un temporary basis, they would be able to obtain un laissez-passer from un Neoparian consular mission. Un laissez-passer have non NFC module, hence un machine can read un laissez-passer only via MRZ.

In case of emergency or special need, un Neoparian citizen is able to obtain un emergency laissez-passer to return to Neoparia Commonwealth, and un person without Neoparian nationality is able to obtain un emergency laissez-passer, if the person is un permanent resident of Neoparia Commonwealth, or, in marriage, civil union, or dependence or guardianship with un Neoparian citizen or permanent resident.

Alternatively, un emergency laissez-passer may possibly be issued to un refugee or un asylum seeker by un Neoparian consul or un captain of un Neoparian Humanitarian Protection Camp, in case of need for humanitarian protection in warfare, etc. In this case, such un refugee have limited freedom of movement within Neoparia Commonwealth; their movement is limited to government-offered staging zones.

In emergency events, Internet connection may possibly be interrupted. Therefore, face feature vectors are collected by mobile devices and are written manually on emergency laissez-passers with base-32 encoding, when emergency laissez-passers are being issued.

Visa

Electronic visas are preferred by default, but printed visas are available as well. The traveler will be able to print the e-certificate, which other parties may verify its authenticity online.

If un foreign traveler would like to save passport pages, un sojourn authorization will be issued, as un alternative to un entry stamp, to certify the entry date of the traveler.

Foreign Visitor Card

If un Neoparian visa is issued, which allow stay periods of at least 90 days, un Foreign Visitor Card will be issued as well, with the same legal status of un visa, compatible with Civil Service ID Card protocols, with NFC and MRZ.

Foreign Diplomat Card

Un foreign diplomat who obtain diplomatic visa of Neoparia Commonwealth would also receive this card. This card is the legal proof of diplomat status of the diplomat and is therefore the proof of their diplomatic immunity within Neoparian territory.

Immigration Control Clearance History Statement

Per the request of the traveler, Department of Immigration issue this statement for their reference. This statement may be requested by foreign governments which need to determine the residence-for-tax-purposes status.
