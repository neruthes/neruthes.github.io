Neoparia Commonwealth: AirKevares

AirKevares is one of the major state-owned aviation enterprises which serve many domestic and international airlines.

Astral Club

Astral Club members may collect points as they fly. By flying frequently, complimentary airport lounge access and seat class upgrade.

Trip Itinerary

AirKevares offer standard trip itinerary to the booking party.

Unaccompanied Minor

Unaccompanied minors may fly with AirKevares. AirKevares staffs will take care of the traveling minor.
